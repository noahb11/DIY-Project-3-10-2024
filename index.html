What is the Amazing Burger?

One of my favorite hamburgers is the Kentucky Bourbon Classic from Mr. Brew's Taphouse.

It's one of my favorites because the sauce used isn't too sweet, and the onion straws give the burger a very fun texture. The two work together with the bacon and cheese to make a truly unique flavor.

Here is what comes on the burger:

Cheddar cheese, grilled onions, two slices of bacon, French-fried onions and Kentucky bourbon mayo.

    The burger costs $10.99 before tax.
